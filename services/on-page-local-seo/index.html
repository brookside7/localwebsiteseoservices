Location Page Strategy

Location pages are critical for multi-location businesses or businesses serving multiple cities. Each page must be unique and valuable to avoid duplicate content issues.

Elements of Effective Location Pages

  • Unique introductions mentioning specific neighborhoods, landmarks, or local context
  • Service details relevant to that location
  • Nearby areas served to capture surrounding searches
  • Local proof elements like testimonials or case studies from that area
  • Embedded map and clear directions
  • Local FAQs addressing area-specific questions
  • Clear conversion paths with phone numbers and forms
