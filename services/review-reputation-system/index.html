How to Handle Negative Reviews

Negative reviews happen. The way you respond matters more than the review itself.

Our Approach to Negative Reviews

  • Fast response within 24-48 hours shows you care
  • Acknowledge the issue without admitting fault prematurely
  • Move offline for resolution discussions
  • Follow up publicly after resolution
  • Learn and improve from legitimate complaints

Negative reviews handled well can actually strengthen trust. Prospective customers want to see that you address problems professionally.

Review Generation Best Practices

1

Right Timing

Request reviews when customer satisfaction is highest, typically right after service completion or delivery.

2

Make It Easy

Direct links to review platforms remove friction. Mobile-optimized flows increase completion.

3

Don't Filter

Never gate review requests or only ask satisfied customers. This violates platform policies.

4

Be Consistent

Systematic requesting generates steady review flow. Consistency beats occasional campaigns.
